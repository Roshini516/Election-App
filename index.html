State Assembly Election Portal

Welcome to the Digital Election System

This platform is designed to ensure a transparent, secure, and efficient voting process for the State Assembly Elections. Every citizen can register, verify credentials, and cast their vote from the comfort of their home using this digital election portal.

Beginner’s Guide to Using This Portal

  1. Register: Create your account using your valid Voter ID and details.
  2. Login: Access the voter section using your credentials.
  3. Vote: Select your candidate and cast your vote securely.
  4. Check Results: Once voting ends, visit the Results section for updates.
  5. Admins: Authorized officials can access the admin panel for system management.

India International Institute of Democracy and Election Management (IIIDEM)

The Election Commission of India (ECI) established the India International Institute of Democracy and Election Management (IIIDEM) to advance its professional competence in election management, promote people's participation, contribute to developing stronger democratic institutions, and support the efforts of ECI in carrying out its mandate and functions.

Over the last six decades, the structure and functions of the Election Commission have undergone major changes. The management of elections has become increasingly complex due to the rising number of political parties, changing dynamics of coalitions and alliances, frequent elections and bye-elections, increase in number of eligible voters, and continuous updating of electoral rolls. This has led to new challenges for election management bodies today.
